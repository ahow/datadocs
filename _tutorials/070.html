Redirect to selection screen

At the end of the run, you will be redirected to “_all_1”-profile_detail.

To make things slightly more user-friendly, actions have been defined (at the top left of the screen, you can see a dropdown-box actions). Basically you can do mass-updates with that ((un)check all the boxes at once, (un)check all the boxes at once for one scraper, (un)check all the boxes at once for all the metrics/scrapers, etc. Additional actions can be added, if needed. The actions with the scraper involve an intermediary screen, which allows you to select the scraper.

The last action is to export the data (after you have made your selection and after you have saved the changes).
